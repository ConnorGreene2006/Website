Explore the World of Martial Arts

Welcome to beyond MMA where each course we provide outstanding insight on the inner workings of MMA and its methods. Ever since 2022, a group of MMA competitors located in Gatineau wanted to show the world their knowledge and hand it on to future generations by opening a school of MMA titles “Beyond MMA”. As of now, Beyond MMA’s courses consist of: Muay Thai, Wrestling, Karate, BJJ, & Boxing.


Your Path to Excellence

At Beyond MMA, our unwavering commitment is to guide you on your path to reaching unparalleled levels of martial arts excellence. Our comprehensive website offers a diverse array of courses meticulously curated to cater to various interests and skill levels. From the dynamic world of striking disciplines to the intricate strategies of ground-based combat, each course is crafted to provide a holistic, challenging, and empowering training experience!
